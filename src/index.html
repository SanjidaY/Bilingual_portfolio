About Me

I am a Data Engineer and Researcher based in Dresden, Germany, holding an M.Sc. in Distributed Systems Engineering from TU Dresden. My core expertise lies in designing robust data architectures, automating complex ETL workflows, and bridging legacy enterprise systems with modern cloud and analytical environments.

In my recent work at JOYNEXT GmbH and the Helmholtz-Zentrum Dresden, I have built automated pipelines orchestrating data flow between SAP SOAP Web Services, PostgreSQL, Oracle RDBMS, and MSSQL. Beyond core engineering, my research background in advanced analytics and machine learning (XGBoost, BERT) enables me to extract predictive insights from industrial IoT and PLC data, directly reducing production downtime.
